Przelewy24 has introduced BLIK Pay Later, a new deferred payment method that lets e-shoppers buy a product without paying immediately using only a 6-digit BLIK code, with the money going to the seller right away while the buyer manages repayment through their bank's app. The service was implemented in May for customers of Millennium and VeloBank, with more banks to be added soon, and is positioned to increase basket value and conversions.
